What is Eight Five Collective

A platform built from years behind the lens. Eight Five Collective gives photographers customizable shot lists, posing prompts, and session checklists — so you can stay present, stay confident, and never miss a moment that matters.

What's Inside
01
Wedding Shot List
A complete shot list organized by category — portraits, details, candids — across every part of the day. With posing directions, camera settings, and a pun to break the tension.
02
Bridal Flatlay Checklist
Send clients a polished, interactive checklist of every detail to bring to their session. No more missed rings, veils, or invitation suites.
03
Your Brand, Your Voice
Add your studio name, customize the categories, edit the puns, remove what you don't need. Every tool should feel like it was made for you — because it was.
04
Built for the Field
Designed to work on any phone, any device, in any light. Pull it up between shots, hand it to an assistant, or send the link to your client the morning of.
